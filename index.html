Computer Room Rules


- Respect & Listen to the staff around you.

- No food or drinks allowed inside the room.

- Please do not run around the computers.

- If you would like to talk please whisper, the computer room should be a quiet place.

- Always make sure you're playing on your own account, and log out before leaving.

- Remember to clean up your area before you leave, no one likes a messy room.

- Never give your personal information to anyone online.
