Computer Room Rules

1. Respect & listen to the staff around you.

2. No food or drinks allowed inside the room.

3. Please do not run around the computers.

4. If you would like to talk, please be quiet.
We shouldn't have to yell over each other.

5. Always make sure you're playing on your
own account, and log out before leaving.

6. Remember to clean up your area before
you leave, no one likes a messy room.

7. Never give out your personal information
to anyone online.
